Slack Macros Bookmarklet

Press Ctrl+Shift+B to show your bookmarks toolbar if you're using Chrome or Internet Explorer. In Firefox, right-click the toolbar and click Bookmarks Toolbar. Just drag and drop the following link to your bookmark toolbar. Slack Macros The bookmarklet is now installed.

To use, just click the bookmarklet after you have opened slack in your browser to load the macros into the browser (note that the macros will be unloaded when you leave the page, so remember to do this every time you load Slack). Hit the Tab key to expand any registered macros.

Registered macros:

  • /frogonacci <number> Expands to a fibonacci sequence of frogs.
